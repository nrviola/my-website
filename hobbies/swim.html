Swimming

TCNJ's Men's Varsity Swim Team (DIII NCAA)

I currently swim on the TCNJ's Men's Varsity Swim Team. TCNJ is a Division III NCAA team that competes in the New Jersey Athletic Conference (NJAC). This team has taught me the importance of leadership and self-impact. I wouldn't trade my time on this team for anything else.

Fishers High School Varsity Swim Team (2022 State Finalist)

I swam for Fishers High School's Varsity Swim Team while I was in high school. In 2022, I placed 17th in the 500 yard freestyle at our IHSAA State Meet. During my time on this team, I met lifelong friends and learned a lot about myself and my love for the sport of swimming.
