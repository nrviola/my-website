Traveling

Over the past 21 years that I've been alive, I've been extremely fortunate to travel all across the US and dabble into many countries around the world. None of this would have been possible without my mom, Elyse Viola. She's been an avid traveler since she was little and has been kind even to include me in her experiences as well. These experience have broadened my perspective on different cultures and love for the world we live in.

States

Throughout the years, I've been to 34 states and the capital of the US. My favorite part about travel the states is observing how different each state truly is. No matter what state you go to, you can find something unique and interesting about them. Whether you're on the mountainous west coast to the beachy east, I guarantee each state has a beauty of its own!
