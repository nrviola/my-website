About Me

As a computer engineering student, I have always been fascinated by the changing face of technology and its potential to change the world. Recent breakthroughs in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Automation have opened up unprecedented opportunities, especially in innovation. I find it very interesting how AI has revolutionized industries from healthcare to environmental sustainability and how I can contribute to these groundbreaking changes.

I have a background in developing hardware and software, with an enthusiasm for devising creative solutions to complex problems. Be it designing efficient systems or implementing computational tools, I am always anxious to attack problems with my mixture of technical expertise and creative thinking.

When I'm not in the field of engineering, I may be in the water: an active and proud member of the College of New Jersey's Men's Varsity Swim Team or exploring new places for my love of travel. Such pursuits fuel my determination, adaptability, and commitment to excellence both within and outside of the pool.
